What I learned: Jul 12-18, 2022

Performance Interview

This week I continued studying for my performance interview, I reviewed agile practices. I learned about scrum actors, the sprint cycle, and the ceremonies of scrum. I also learned more about testing, and the difference between unit testing, integration tests, and end to end tests. I also learned about recursion, sorting and algorithms. I learned about the breadth first algorithm, quicksort and binary search. I also learned about IP directions, domains, DNS and started reading about design patterns.

The interview mania activity is also helping me study and review for the performance interview, as most questions I'm preparing to ask are about the same topics I've been studying: computer science fundamentals.

Cracking the Coding Interview & interview practice videos

I learned more about the interview process for developers. I learned how interviews can be, some questions we should be prepared to answer, different questions or areas to study depending on the position you’re interviewing for. I also learned more about the way the practical part of an interview can progress and some tips on how to act on them.

Hacker Rank

The Hacker Rank challenges have helped me to practice more and see different solutions to the same problem. I haven’t gone through as many challenges as I'd like, because I've been trying to focus on studying and understanding the topics, it worries me that I won’t be able to finish them all.
